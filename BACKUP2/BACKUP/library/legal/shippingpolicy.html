Shipping and Delivery Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2022

This Shipping and Delivery Policy is part of our ToS and should be read alongside our main Terms: Terms of Service.

Please review our Shipping and Delivery Policy when purchasing our products. This policy will apply to any order you place with us.

Shipping and Delivery Options

We offer multiple shipping options. A third-party supplier may be managing our inventory and will be responsible for shipping your product.

Order Delays

If delivery is delayed for any reason, we will do our best to let you know as soon as possible and will estimate a revised delivery date. We have no control over any delays and wish to get you your purchase as soon as possible.

Expected Shipping Time

Shipping within the US is approximately 1-2 weeks after order fulfillment. All items are made to order and are placed in a queue. Most queue items are fulfilled within 4 days. We do not ship outside the US.

Questions About Returns

If you have questions about returns or refunds, please review our Return Policy.
